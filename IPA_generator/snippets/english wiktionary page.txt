<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adjective">Adjective</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=excellent&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Adjective">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">excellent</strong> (<i><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#comparative" title="Appendix:Glossary">comparative</a></i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en comparative-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/excellenter#English" title="excellenter">excellenter</a></b> <i>or</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en comparative-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/more#English" title="more">more</a> excellent</b>, <i><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#superlative" title="Appendix:Glossary">superlative</a></i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en superlative-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/excellentest#English" title="excellentest">excellentest</a></b> <i>or</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en superlative-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/most#English" title="most">most</a> excellent</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>Having <a href="/wiki/excelled" title="excelled">excelled</a>, having <a href="/wiki/surpassed" title="surpassed">surpassed</a>.</li>
<li>Of higher or the highest <a href="/wiki/quality" title="quality">quality</a>; <a href="/wiki/splendid" title="splendid">splendid</a>.
<ul><li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>1913</b>,  <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marie_Belloc_Lowndes" class="extiw" title="w:Marie Belloc Lowndes">Mrs. [Marie] Belloc Lowndes</a>,  chapter I, in  <cite><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Lodger_(novel)" class="extiw" title="w:The Lodger (novel)">The Lodger</a></cite>, London&#58; <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Methuen_Publishing" class="extiw" title="w:Methuen Publishing">Methuen</a>, <small><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OCLC" class="extiw" title="w:OCLC">OCLC</a> <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://worldcat.org/oclc/7780546">7780546</a></small>&#59; republished in  <cite>Novels of Mystery: The Lodger; The Story of Ivy; What Really Happened</cite>, New York, N.Y.&#58; <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Longman" class="extiw" title="w:Longman">Longmans, Green and Co.</a>,<span class="q-hellip-sp">&#160;</span><span class="q-hellip-b">[</span><span title="55 Fifth Avenue">…</span><span class="q-hellip-b">]</span><span class="q-hellip-b"></span>, [1933], <small><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OCLC" class="extiw" title="w:OCLC">OCLC</a> <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://worldcat.org/oclc/2666860">2666860</a></small>, <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://archive.org/stream/novelsofmysteryl00lown#page/n25/mode/1up">page 0016</a>:</span><dl><dd></dd></dl></div>
<dl><dd>A great bargain also had been the <b>excellent</b> Axminster carpet which covered the floor; as, again, the arm-chair in which Bunting now sat forward, staring into the dull, small fire.</dd></dl></li></ul></li>
<li>Exceptionally good of its <a href="/wiki/kind" title="kind">kind</a>.
<ul><li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>2013</b> July-August,  <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.americanscientist.org/authors/detail/catherine-clabby">Catherine Clabby</a>,  “<a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.americanscientist.org/issues/pub/2013/4/focus-on-everything">Focus on Everything</a>”, in  <cite><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Scientist" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:American Scientist">American Scientist</a></cite>:</span><dl><dd><div class="h-quotation"><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="en">Not long ago, it was difficult to produce photographs of tiny creatures with every part in focus. <dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">Bill and Ted had an <b>excellent</b> adventure last week in preparation of their history exam.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li>Superior in kind or degree, irrespective of moral quality.
